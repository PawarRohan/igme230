Thought At Work 2018 Title Sequence

Role:

Graphics direction, Title Sequence design, Title Sequence animation

Duration:

3 months

Overview:

For the title sequence for this year, we chose to go through the process that goes on as an idea goes from an inkling of a thought, to a fully fledged idea ready to hit the canvas. We follow the idea through the bumps in the road, and the twists and turns it goes through before becoming a concept.
