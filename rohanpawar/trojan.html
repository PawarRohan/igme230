ROHAN PAWAR

Trojan Condoms Concept Advertisement

Role:

Photo Manipulation, Visual Direction

Duration:

3 weeks

Overview:

This concept advertisement campaign was an exercise in following branding identity and visual direction. I challenged myself to attempt to follow Trojan Condoms’ current identity in media. The advertisements they’ve made tend to be based on humor, playing into the taboo-ness that comes with sex related topics. A lot of their advertisements can be subtle or relatively “in your face”. I chose to explore the second route.
